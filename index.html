Our Services

Birth Chart Reading

A birth chart reading (also called a natal chart or astrological chart interpretation) provides insights into your personality, strengths, challenges, and life path based on the positions of celestial bodies at your exact time, date, and place of birth.

Numerology Report

A numerology report provides insights into your life path, personality, and destiny based on the vibrational energies of numbers derived from your birth date and name.

Lunar Guidance

Lunar guidance (also called Moon phase astrology or Moon cycle wisdom) uses the ever-changing phases of the Moon to help you align with natural rhythms for manifestation, emotional balance, and intuitive growth.

Compatibility Analysis

Compatibility analysis (also called synastry in astrology or relationship numerology) examines the energetic harmony between two individuals—whether romantic partners, friends, family, or business associates. It reveals strengths, challenges, and growth opportunities in the relationship.

Yearly Forecast

A yearly report (also called an annual horoscope, solar return, or personal year forecast) provides a roadmap of the major themes, opportunities, and challenges you’re likely to encounter in the coming year. It blends astrological transits, numerology cycles, and personal growth insights to help you navigate the year with clarity and purpose.

Personalized Remedies

A personalized remedies report provides tailored spiritual, energetic, and practical solutions to balance challenges in your birth chart, numerology, or life circumstances. These remedies draw from astrology, gemology, Feng Shui, mantra healing, and other esoteric traditions to harmonize your energy and attract positive outcomes.
